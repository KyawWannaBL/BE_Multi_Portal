Enterprise Portals (11 Total)
These portals serve as the primary entry points for different departments and external partners:

Admin Portal (/portal/admin): Reserved for high-level system oversight.

Operations Portal (/portal/operations): Central hub for shipment management and tracking.

Finance Portal (/portal/finance): For managing corporate accounts and transactions.

Execution Portal (/portal/execution): Designed for logistics personnel like riders and drivers.

HR Portal (/portal/hr): Manages employee records and organizational structure.

Marketing Portal (/portal/marketing): For promotional and growth-related data.

Support Portal (/portal/support): Used by customer service agents.

Supervisor Portal (/portal/supervisor): For overseeing regional or team operations.

Warehouse Portal (/portal/warehouse): Specifically for stock and inventory management.

Branch Portal (/portal/branch): For substation and branch managers.

Merchant/Customer Portals (/portal/merchant, /portal/customer): External interfaces for clients and partners.

🛠️ Legacy Admin Platform
In addition to the modern portals, there is a Legacy Admin Route (/admin) that contains deep-level management modules:

SuperAdmin Dashboard: Overall system health.

Account Control: Managing user approvals and access.

Fleet Command: Oversight of delivery vehicles.

Omni Finance: Advanced financial controls.

Live Map: Real-time tracking of all active shipments.

🔐 Access and Security
Portal Redirection: Upon a successful login via the L5 Secure Gateway, the system automatically identifies your role and directs you to the correct portal.

Authorization Requirement: Every portal is protected by a RequireRole guard. For example, a user with the RIDER role can access the Execution Portal but will be blocked from the Finance Portal.

Bilingual Support: All portals support the English/Myanmar toggle to accommodate different regional users.
